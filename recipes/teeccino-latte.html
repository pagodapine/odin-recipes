Ingredients

  • Favorite Teeccino tea bag
  • Water
  • Favorite milk or milk alternative

Steps:

  1. Heat water to 208 F.
  2. Pour water in favorite vessel.
  3. Add favorite Teeccino teabag for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Top off with favorite milk or milk alternative.
  5. Enjoy with good company.
